Compassionate Home Care Services in Houston, Texas

Primetime Home Health Services, Inc. proudly provides professional home care throughout the greater Houston metropolitan area. Our experienced caregivers support seniors, veterans, and families who wish to remain comfortable and independent in their homes.

Why Choose Houston Home Care from Primetime?

  • Local expertise: Houston-based caregivers who understand the unique needs of the community.
  • Flexible schedules: From a few hours of companionship to 24-hour live-in care, we adapt to your needs.
  • Trusted professionals: Background-checked, trained, and compassionate caregivers.
  • Insurance accepted: Medicaid, Medicare Advantage, and most major private plans.

Services We Provide in Houston

  • Personal assistance with bathing, dressing, grooming
  • Medication reminders and medication management
  • Respite care for family caregivers
  • Companionship and meaningful engagement
  • Light housekeeping and meal preparation
  • Transportation to medical appointments
  • Alzheimer's and dementia care support
  • Veteran care services

Coverage Areas in Houston

We serve all major Houston neighborhoods and surrounding communities including:

  • Downtown Houston & Midtown
  • Memorial, River Oaks, and West University
  • Montrose, Heights, and Rice Village
  • Southwest Houston, Bellaire, and Meyerland
  • Clear Lake, Friendswood, and Pearland areas
  • Katy, Cypress, and Tomball
  • The Woodlands and Spring
